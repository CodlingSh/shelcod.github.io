Hello there, and welcome to my webpage.

Since 2012, I have been an avid programmer. I stand at the front of the fastest moving technology trend in the world today- computer programming. Since I was four years old, I have always had a soft spot for computers. Memories of our family-owned 486-based PC are still fresh in my mind, as it gave me my first exposure to the world of computers. From those humble beginnings, I developed a deep understanding of several Linux distributions and Windows. Consequently, my love for programming has been growing ever since.

My computer-relevant educational background dates back to 2013, where I took the computer network specialist course at Lambton College, where I studied system repair and computer maintenance. But currently, I am a second-year computer programming student. My formal training has laid a solid foundation that I hope to build on in my programming journey. I have gained an in-depth understanding of HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery, Python, and SQL. Additionally, I have acquired self-taught skills in Lua, C, and Bash.

For me, the sky is the limit, and I hope to live to embrace the core values of innovation and technology. When I’m not studying or coding, I enjoy playing video games, graphic design, programming, watching movies, mountain biking, chess, and toying around with Linux distributions.

Thanks for taking the time to get to know me better. If you have any questions for me, please send me an email at shelcod@gmail.com or check out my contact page to follow me on social media.
